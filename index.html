About Us

Welcome to Kvernufall, a hotel nestled next to the breathtaking Kvernufoss waterfall in Iceland. Our hotel offers an efficient solution for those seeking a peaceful and eco-friendly getaway.

Our rooms are designed to cater to the needs of families, couples, and solo travelers. We offer a variety of room types ranging from standard rooms to more luxurious accommodations for couples seeking a romantic escape. Each room is equipped with all the necessary amenities to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

At Kvernufall, we pride ourselves on being environmentally conscious. Our hotel is designed to minimize our carbon footprint, and we use sustainable materials and energy sources to operate. We believe in preserving the beauty of Iceland's nature and strive to protect it for future generations.

Our location is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts. You can explore the waterfall, hike in the surrounding mountains, and enjoy various outdoor activities such as bird watching, fishing, and horseback riding. The hotel is located in a serene environment, providing a perfect getaway from the hustle and bustle of the city.

We understand that planning a trip can be stressful, which is why we offer various packages to make your stay at Kvernufall hassle-free. Our packages include accommodation, meals, and exciting activities such as glacier tours, hot spring visits, and more.

Book your stay at Kvernufall today and experience the beauty of Iceland's nature in an eco-friendly and comfortable setting. We invite you to take advantage of our current offer and receive a 10% discount on your first booking. Hurry and book now!

Rooms

Family-Friendly Rooms

Family-Friendly Rooms

Our family-friendly rooms offer spacious and comfortable accommodation suitable for families of all sizes. These rooms feature large, comfortable beds, a sitting area, and a private bathroom. In addition, we offer in-room amenities such as free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV with children's channels, a mini fridge for storing snacks, and a safe for your valuables. Cribs or extra beds can also be arranged upon request. Our goal is to provide a comfortable and enjoyable stay for you and your family at Kvernufall.

Price: $200 per night

Romantic Getaway Rooms

Romantic Getaway Rooms

For couples seeking a romantic escape, we offer our Romantic Getaway Rooms. These rooms provide a cozy and intimate setting with a private balcony offering stunning views of the Kvernufoss waterfall. The rooms are furnished with a plush king-size bed, a private hot tub, and a fireplace. In addition, we provide room service, a bottle of champagne upon arrival, and a romantic dinner option for two. Experience a memorable romantic stay in the heart of Iceland's natural beauty at Kvernufall.

Price: $300 per night
